The Practice

Lenina Rassool is a journalist, communications strategist, and multimedia producer with 20 years of experience spanning mainstream media and social justice. She has worked in communications for numerous organisations with a focus on civic engagement, community news and gender justice. Her work has appeared in Femina, Cosmopolitan, the Cape Times, and the Mail & Guardian.

Her practice sits at the border of journalism and systems thinking. She positions communication as both a civic act and a systemic intervention, helping audiences see themselves as participants in larger webs of responsibility and possibility.
